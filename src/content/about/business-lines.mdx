    **Use**  
    To pay for supplies or services.

    ## Travel

    **Use**  
    To pay for travel and travel related expenses. Primary mechanism used to purchase airline, rail and bus tickets at significantly reduced fares under the [GSA City Pair Program](https://www.gsa.gov/travel/plan-a-trip/transportation-airfare-rates-pov-rates-etc/airfare-rates-city-pair-program) (CPP).  

    **Account Type(s)**  
    Individually Billed Account (IBA) or CBA.

    **Publication**  
    [Helpful Hints for Travel Account Use](/about/business-lines/helpful-hints/travel-account).

  </div>
</BusinessCard>

    ## Fleet

    **Use**  
    To pay for the purchase of fuel, maintenance and repair of government owned/operated motor equipment, aviation, small marine vessels and large marine vessels.

    **Account Type**  
    CBA.  

    **Publication**  
    [Helpful Hints for Fleet Account Use](/about/business-lines/helpful-hints/fleet-account).   

  </div>
</BusinessCard>

  <div slot="right">
    ## Integrated

    **Use**  
    To pay for purchases involving two or more business lines (i.e. fleet, purchase, and/or travel) on a single account.

    **Account Type**  
    Most times considered to be a CBA and should be exempt from state sales tax.

    **Publications**  
    [Helpful Hints for Purchase Account Use](/about/business-lines/helpful-hints/purchase-account), [Helpful Hints for Travel Account Use](/about/business-lines/helpful-hints/travel-account) and [Helpful Hints for Fleet Account Use](/about/business-lines/helpful-hints/fleet-account).
      
  </div>
</BusinessCard>
