## Background

According to the General Services Administration's SmartPay statistics, during FY 2023, the DoD's GTCC program spent $6.4 billion and had 2.4 million individually billed accounts and 9,457 centrally billed accounts. Citi provides an online EAS to assist users in the management of the DoD GTCC program.

## Recommendations

We made 21 recommendations to the Director, Defense Support Services Report for review, develop training, and coordinate with Citi on mandatory report issues.
